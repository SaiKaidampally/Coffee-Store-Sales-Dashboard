# Coffee Store Sales Dashboard – Mini Project Report

## Problem Statement
Retail coffee stores need insight into sales trends to optimize inventory and increase revenue.

## Methodology
1. Data Loading
2. Data Cleaning
3. Feature Engineering
4. Aggregation & Analysis
5. Data Visualization
6. Business Insight Generation

## Visualizations Created
1.	Trend of transactions and sales by day of the week (Monday–Sunday)
2.	Revenue distribution by coffee type
3.	Payment method preference (card vs. cash)
4.	Average daily transactions and average daily sales
5.	Peak times of the day: morning, afternoon, evening

## Observations
- Peak sales are observed in certain months indicating seasonal demand.
- Coffee and Bakery categories contribute maximum revenue.
- Few products dominate overall sales indicating focus inventory items.

## Conclusion
The dashboard enables business managers to make informed decisions on inventory planning, promotional strategies, and demand forecasting.
